UC Santa Cruz is my current top pick in terms of colleges. It has easy access to the beach and it is in a great part of California. Its location allows for access to some of the larger cities for the tech industry which could allow for future jobs or internships. The campus is really unique being shrouded by a grove of redwood trees. They have a good computer science program without the pressure of a top university.
